This website takes your privacy very seriously. This policy explains how we collect, process, and protect your personal information when you use our services.

#### Scope

This privacy policy applies to the collection, use, and protection of personal data when you use this website. It does not apply to:
- External linked websites
- Personnel not commissioned or managed by this website

#### Collection and Use of Personal Data

**Information We Collect**

When you use interactive features on this website, we may collect the following information:
- Name
- Email address
- Contact information
- Other information you voluntarily provide

**Server Logs**

For website management and service improvement, our servers automatically record related information, including:
- IP address
- Access time
- Browser type
- Pages visited
- User behavior

**Purpose of Use**

**Without your written consent, this website will not use your personal data for any other purpose.** The personal data we collect is used solely for:
- Providing the services you request
- Contacting you
- Improving the quality of our services
- Conducting website usage analysis

#### Data Protection

**Security Measures**

This website employs the following measures to protect your personal data:
- Firewall systems
- Data encryption technology
- Access control mechanisms

**Personnel Management**

Only authorized personnel have access to your personal data, and all relevant personnel have signed confidentiality agreements. Violations are subject to legal penalties.

#### Third-Party Data Sharing

**This website will never provide, exchange, rent, or sell any of your personal data to third parties.**

Exceptions are limited to:
- With your written consent
- As required by law
- When necessary to protect public interest or the legitimate rights of this website
- For statistical or academic research by research institutions in the public interest (data processed to prevent identification of specific individuals)

#### External Links

This website may contain links to other websites. These external websites have their own privacy policies, and this website's privacy policy does not apply to those externally linked sites.

#### Use of Cookies

To provide better services, this website may use cookies technology. You can choose to refuse cookies through your browser settings, but this may affect the functionality of certain website features.

#### Policy Revisions

This website reserves the right to revise this privacy policy at any time. When significant changes are made to our privacy policy, updates will be posted on the website.
