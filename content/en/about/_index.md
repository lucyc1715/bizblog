## Hi, I'm Lucy

A **frontend engineer, career consultant, and music enthusiast**.

I believe engineers can do more than just write code — combining knowledge from different fields makes us stronger.

## My Story

I love exploring knowledge across different industries. I believe technology is a professional skill, but combining it with cross-disciplinary industry knowledge is what drives us toward our true calling.
From startups to multinational banks, from Taiwan to the UK, I've found my rhythm in diverse environments.

My specialty is **cross-disciplinary communication** — bridging the gap between engineers and users, translating complex technical jargon into language everyone can understand. It's not just a skill; it's my passion.
